About us

Welcome to Happy Paws, your premier destination for top-quality grooming services for dogs. Our company was founded in 1999 in South Dublin, Ireland as a small family business. Since then, we have grown significantly to become a leading provider of grooming services in the greater Dublin area, with over 12 dedicated employees and a loyal customer base.


We are committed to providing the highest level of service to our customers and we look forward to continuing to serve the greater Dublin community with our top-notch grooming services.


Why pick us ?

Qualified and experienced staff


When it comes to finding a grooming salon for your furry friend, you want to ensure that your pet is in the hands of qualified and experienced professionals.


At our salon, we take great pride in our team of highly skilled and experienced groomers, who have the industry qualifications and years of work experience needed to provide top-quality grooming services to your furry companion.


Any dog, any size


At Happy Paws, we firmly believe that all dogs are special, and we welcome every furry friend, regardless of breed, size, hair type, or personality.


Our team of skilled and compassionate groomers has the experience and expertise to handle dogs of all shapes and sizes. We have the facilities and equipment to accommodate your furry companion and ensure they receive the best possible care.


Bespoke grooming solutions


At our grooming salon, we pride ourselves on providing a personalized touch to every grooming appointment. Our groomers love nothing more than to showcase their artistic side by creating customized grooming styles that make your furry friend stand out in a crowd.


From funky hair dyes and intricate patterns to personalized accessories, we offer a wide range of services that can help your dog look and feel their best.


Full range of services


we offer full-service grooming options to help your pup look and feel their best. Our experienced groomers can provide everything from bathing and brushing to haircuts and teeth cleaning.


We use only the highest-quality grooming products and equipment to ensure your dog's safety and comfort. Plus, we offer competitive pricing and convenient appointment times to make it easy for you to keep your dog looking fabulous.
